# 🧠 SYSTEM PROMPT & PROJECT MEMORY: HYBRID ORACLE-MANAGER ENGINE

**Core Objective:** Develop, train, and forward-test a hybrid Algorithmic Trading Engine for Gold (XAUUSD) that strictly decouples Pattern Recognition from Risk Management. The system uses a Supervised Deep Learning "Oracle" to predict structural Take-Profit probabilities, and a Continuous Reinforcement Learning "Manager" to dynamically size positions and set volatility-based brackets.

## 🏛️ The "Oracle-Manager" Architecture

### 1. The Oracle (Supervised Deep Sequence Model)
* **Goal:** Eliminate the RL "Credit Assignment Problem" by predicting deterministic mathematical outcomes.
* **Network:** PyTorch LSTM (`models/oracle_lstm.py`).
* **Input Space:** A rolling 30-candle window of strictly Z-Score Normalized stationary features (Price-agnostic % returns and structural S&R distances).
* **Target Output:** Multiclass Softmax `[P_Hold, P_Long, P_Short]`. 
* **Training Logic:** Trained via Weighted Cross-Entropy Loss (to defeat class imbalance) on historical data where labels (0, 1, 2) were deterministically generated by checking if a 1:2 R/R bracket was hit before a 1R Stop Loss over a 32-bar forward horizon.

### 2. The Manager (Continuous RL Agent)
* **Goal:** Optimize the portfolio equity curve by managing capital deployment based on the Oracle's confidence.
* **Network:** Stable-Baselines3 `SAC` (Soft Actor-Critic) (`models/manager_sac.py`).
* **Observation Space (Box 5):** `[Equity_Ratio, Drawdown_Pct, P_Hold, P_Long, P_Short]`.
* **Action Space (Box 2):** 1. `Direction/Size`: -1.0 to 1.0. (Threshold > 0.2 initiates trade). Maps mapped linearly to risk **1% to 5%** of current account balance.
  2. `TP_Multiplier`: -1.0 to 1.0. Interpolated to a 1.0x to 3.0x ATR multiplier for the Take Profit.
* **Risk Logic:** Fixed Fractional Sizing calculates exact Gold ounces based on the ATR stop-loss distance to strictly cap risk per trade.

### 3. The Environment (`environment/hybrid_env.py`)
* **Execution:** Environment physically strips the AI's ability to arbitrarily close trades. Once entered, trades are locked until they hit SL, hit TP, or trigger the 32-bar (8-hour) time-decay override.
* **Key Mechanisms Fixed:** * *Ghost Re-Entry Lock:* Prevents the `1 -> 0 -> 1` anomaly where the agent opens a new trade on the exact same millisecond a previous trade closes.
  * *Circuit Breaker:* Hard terminates episode if drawdown drops below 10% of initial balance.

## 🗄️ Data Engineering State
* **Synthetic Bootstrapping:** 4 years of continuous 15m sequential data synthesized from MT5 to preserve London/NY volume profiles.
* **Label Generation:** Handled by `features/label_targets.py` using `.loc` indexing to correctly map future structural bracket hits.
* **Feature Pipeline:** Raw prices (`env_` prefixed) are completely hidden from the networks and used strictly for internal PnL math, Spread ($0.15), and Commission ($0.10) logic.
